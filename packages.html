PACKAGES

What are packages?

The gym offers a range of packages tailored to meet diverse fitness needs and preferences. The 1-month plan provides a flexible option for those wanting to explore the gym or pursue short-term fitness goals, while the 3-month plan offers a slight discount and suits individuals seeking a medium-term commitment. For those prioritizing longer-term fitness journeys, the 6-month plan provides greater savings and commitment, while the 12-month plan offers the most cost-effective option per month, ideal for dedicated individuals looking for substantial savings and a full year of personalized training. Each package is designed to cater to varying levels of commitment and budget, empowering individuals to choose the plan that aligns best with their fitness aspirations and lifestyle.
